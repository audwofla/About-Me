ABOUT ME

Jeremy Myung is a violinist from California. He was accepted into Andrews University in 2018 with the Dare to Dream scholarship. Jeremy was a member of the Colburn Chamber Orchestra, Colburn Youth Orchestra, and concertmaster of Temple City High School Honors Orchestra. He was also the concert master of the Andrews University Symphony Orchestra. He has also participated in the Idyllwild Music Festival and Southern California Junior Bach Festival. He has also won competitions such as the Young Artist Competition. He has studied violin under Theresa Woo, Pasha Tseitlin, Cheryl Scheidemantle, and Lyndon Taylor. In his free time, he enjoys playing the guitar, producing music, woodcarving, and playing with his bird Hamlet. After graduating from Andrews University, Jeremy regularly plays for various concerts, weddings, and choirs. In addition to performing, Jeremy also teaches violin to students of all ages.
